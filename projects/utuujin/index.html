ABOUT

UTUUJIN is a pixel adventure puzzle game I made for my Year 11 Software Development and Design Major Project. Because I was limited to using Visual Basic, the rendering was built from the ground up using raster graphics.

The design was heavily inspired by Yume Nikki, another pixel adventure game, attempting to recreate its vibe through the music and aesthetic. Overall, I think it was quite successful, though short and linear, as a game, it's the most thoroughly made out of my projects so far.
