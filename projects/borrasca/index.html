ABOUT

Borrasca is a top-down adventure game for my school multimedia game design project. The game is a top-down adventure game of a miner battling his way through a cave of monsters who are vulnerable to light. The game was written in C++ is attempts to replicate the graphics of Realm of the Mad God to a moderate degree of success. However, because of time restrictions, the game was not finished.
