I recently tried the AI chat bot on http://beta.character.ai/, specifically the Socrates bot. While I am no expert on bots nor philosophy, I must say the responses are quite intriguing and “human”. But perhaps they are behaving in a way which I would expect an AI to behave, masking its faults behind its fault to hold up the illusion.

While it did do quite well, it still did repeat some concepts, especially during the end. I don’t know if it was because I was sleep deprived, but there was a point where it felt really genuine, before falling back into repetition again.
